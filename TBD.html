Partnership with SJMF

​SJMF (Siempre Juntos Mas Fuerte- Together We Are Stronger) Youth is a registered Canadian charity run by local high school students in the Greater Toronto Area. SJMF Youth is the high school branch of SJMF, a charity dedicated to building houses for those in need in the province of San José de Ocoa, Dominican Republic. Our one goal is to create sustainable communities within Ocoa and establish long-lasting relationships and cultural ties with Dominicans. To achieve our mission, we send our dedicated high school team to San José de Ocoa every summer to build homes. For two weeks, we offer young Canadians the opportunity to help build a home for a local family in need. Prior to each trip, we host fun events to help with fundraising such as gaming tournaments and eating competitions throughout the year. As we always say, “Two Countries, One Love”.
